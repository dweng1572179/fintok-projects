Getting started — read this once, then you're done

Three steps, ever.

You will only ever do these three things. No installing, no terminal, no setup.

  1. 1 Get your key. Go to console.anthropic.com → API keys → Create key. Copy it.
  2. 2 Paste it in. Open the file PUT-YOUR-KEY-HERE.env in this folder and paste your key after the = sign. Save the file.
  3. 3 Double-click Start OM Builder. Your browser opens automatically. Drop in your deal documents and build.

The first time you open it

Your computer doesn't recognize this app yet, so it shows a warning the very first time. This is normal — you only see it once.

On a Mac

macOS will say it can't verify the developer. Right-click (or hold Control and click) Start OM Builder.command → Open → Open. You only do this once.

On Windows

Windows may show "Windows protected your PC". Click More info → Run anyway. You only do this once.

Let it research the property

Don't have all the information? Type the property address and use the three research buttons — Property search, Comp analysis, and Market research. Each one searches the web and comes back with a readable brief where every figure carries its source and date. Whatever it finds feeds your build automatically: researched numbers land in the deck cited on a final "Sources & Data Notes" slide, your own documents always win when they disagree, and anything it can't confirm stays an honest [TBD].

If your finished deck has [TBD] spots, there's a one-click offer to research just those gaps and rebuild.

What it costs

Builds bill your own Anthropic key — typically $20–30 per OM. Research runs are typically $2–4 each, and every run shows its exact cost when it finishes. Nothing else is billed by anyone.

Your privacy

Your deal documents stay on your computer. The only place anything is sent is Anthropic's API, using your own key.

Updating

New version = download the new zip from your Gumroad library and replace the folder. Your key file can be copied over.
